Wellness Experience AT RAMSUKH RESORTS AND SPA

Nature-inspired experiences at our wellness hotel in Mahabaleshwar

Experience rejuvenating massages and treatments in one of the best spa in Mahabaleshwar, be one with yourself in nature during the guided yoga & meditation sessions, try your luck at spotting birds or a Shekru (Malabar giant squirrel), and more. Discover endemic & migratory birds on our thoughtfully installed water pots, learn about nature through wildlife documentary screenings, guided birdwatching, cross a water stream and gaze at the Sahyadri mountain views during the daily guided jungle trek in Mahabaleshwar. Enjoy one of the best sightseeing points in Mahabaleshwar during the daily evening valley walks or read from a wide range of topics in our library surrounded by greenery. Get closer to nature and experience the best wellness resort in Mahabaleshwar!

Spa

Indulge in our serene Spa surrounded by lush foliage. Experience rejuvenating therapies, beauty treatments, and facials enhanced by essential oils. Our expert masseurs provide deep relaxation and healing. Enjoy a soothing Jacuzzi experience. Relax body, mind, and soul at our Spa in Mahabaleshwar.

Outdoor fitness zone

Sweat out extra kilos in our outdoor fitness zone amidst the outstanding nature of Kshetra Mahabaleshwar. For tranquillity seekers, our resort offers cool stone and wood sit-outs and benches, ideal for absorbing nature's stillness.

Guided Yoga & Meditation

Embracing the ancient Indian tradition of Yoga and Meditation, Ramsukh Resorts also offers guided Yogain Mahabaleshwar, and Meditation in Mahabaleshwar indoors or in nature, outdoors with views, as you prefer. The session is conducted by a trained professional who guides you through each pose and meditation technique, ensuring that you reap the maximum benefits. These sessions are designed to help you find your inner peace, improve your concentration, and enhance your overall well-being. We also provide quality mats and carpets for the same.

Daily Guided Jungle Trek

For those who love a bit of adventure, we offer a daily guided jungle trek in Mahabaleshwar. Imagine strolling through Kshetra Mahabaleshwar’s lush green forest - historically known as “BramhaAranya”, crossing a plateau full of Fern plants with views of the Sahyadri mountain range, crossing a water stream deep inside the forest which is the origin of multiple rivers, listening to the harmonious symphony of nature, spotting wild orchid plants and breathing in the fresh air. The daily guided jungle trek at Ramsukh Resorts is not just an adventure activity but also a therapeutic experience that connects you with nature in its purest form.

Birdwatching

Explore the unique birds of Mahabaleshwar at our resort. Spot endemic species like the Nilgiri Wood Pigeon and Malabar Whistling Thrush, along with others like the Indian Paradise Flycatcher. Our valley-side walk offers chances to see the Indian Peregrine Falcon and Black Eagle. Don't forget your binoculars and camera with super zoom! We have a trained birdwatching guide too!

Valley Walk

Our daily guided valley walk offers views of the Krishna Valley and the faraway backwater of a dam. Kate’s point AKA Elephant’s head point is seen on clear days. This site is the best at sunrise, but also very pleasant at sunset!

Library

A library in Mahabaleshwar! Our library cum meeting room is nestled in nature. Browse books across genres, including captivating reads on the environment. Whether seeking solitude or engaging in stimulating conversation, find sanctuary amidst our serene surroundings

Wildlife Documentary Screening

Get to know about local wildlife around Maharashtra and start being more mindful of your environment through the screenings of some local wildlife documentaries daily at Ramsukh Resorts & Spa.

Stroll in Resorts

Take a leisurely stroll through Ramsukh Resorts and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Kshetra Mahabaleshwar. Surrounded by native trees and stunning valley and mountain views, unlike any other hotel in Mahabaleshwar, rejuvenation happens effortlessly as you explore our resort. Listen to the soothing sounds of birdsong echoing through the air, while breathing in the pure mountain air. Located at one of the highest altitudes in Mahabaleshwar, our resort offers a tranquil escape where nature's harmony envelops you at every turn.
