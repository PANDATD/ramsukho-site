CONFERENCES AT RAMSUKH RESORTS AND SPA

A unique conference venue in Mahabaleshwar nestled in the heart of the Western Ghats!

Planning a conference in Mahabaleshwar? Ramsukh Resorts & Spa offers unique surroundings and premium amenities, making it a standout among meeting venues. Whether it's a large conference or a business retreat, our seminar halls in Mahabaleshwar nestled in the heart of western ghats provide a comfortable experience. Look no further if you are looking for a valley view conference venue in Mahabaleshwar. Make your next offbeat corporate event in Mahabaleshwar memorable with us.

Black & Red Hall

Our spacious and versatile Black & Red Hall is designed to cater to various indoor events with a keen eye on aesthetics and acoustics. With the flexibility to host up to 600 people when combined with our outdoor lawn, it's the perfect choice for grand gatherings. Indoor seating capacity ranges from 250-300 people, with a floating capacity of up to 350 people.

Forest Canopy

Set under the serene shade of lush rainforest trees, the Forest Canopy serves as an ideal extension to our main conference hall. A flat floor and an extended deck area make it perfect for buffet-style meals, seating, and various other events. The outdoor seating capacity ranges from a gala dinner style for 120 people to a theatre style for 150-200 people, and a floating style for 250+ people. When combined with the Main Conference Hall and the lawns in front of the rooms, it can host 500+ people across three different venues.

Main Conference Hall

Equipped with top-notch audio systems, even lighting, and a projector screen, our Main Conference Hall is designed to meet your presentation needs. It can comfortably accommodate a maximum of 180 persons theatre style or 100-120 persons in a cluster style/U-shaped setup.

Library Room

For more intimate meetings or conferences, our Small Conference Hall offers soundproof interiors, a large TV screen, and optimal lighting for a productive experience. It can seat up to 20 persons.
